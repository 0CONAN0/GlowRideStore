Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions about our products, orders, and policies.

1. What types of products do you offer?

At GlowRide, we specialize in creating LED and neon art pieces inspired by cars and automotive culture. Our collection includes custom designs that bring your passion for cars to life in a unique and visually captivating way.

2. How can I place an order?

You can place an order directly through our website by browsing our products and adding them to your cart. Once you’ve finished shopping, proceed to checkout to complete your purchase. We currently offer Cash on Delivery for your convenience.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

Currently, we accept Cash on Delivery as the primary payment method. We are working to add more payment options in the future for a smoother shopping experience.

4. How long does shipping take?

Shipping times may vary depending on your location, but generally, you can expect your order to arrive within 5-7 business days after it has been processed. You will receive a tracking number to monitor your order’s progress.

5. What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer support to initiate a return. Items must be in their original condition for a full refund or exchange.

6. Do you offer custom designs?

Yes, we offer custom designs for those who want a unique piece. Contact us with your ideas, and our design team will work with you to create a custom piece tailored to your preferences.
